CORE CONCEPTS

Principles That Transform How You Manage Blood Sugar

The 15-15 Rule

Consume 15g fast carbs, wait 15 minutes, re-test. The emergency protocol that saves lives.

PFF Plate Architecture

Protein, Fat, Fiber — the three structural brakes that prevent post-meal crashes.

The 3.5-Hour Interval Rule

Never go more than 4 hours without fuel. Prevent the stealth descent before it starts.

Nutritional Anchoring

Never eat carbs alone. Always pair with protein or fat to slow glucose absorption.

The Nocturnal Bridge Snack

A slow-digesting pre-bed snack to prevent dangerous midnight crashes.

The 4-7-8 Vagal Reset

A breathing technique to halt stress-induced glucose dumps and restore calm.
